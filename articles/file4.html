(https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Centre_Georges_Pompidou) Centre Georges Pompidou (French pronunciation: ​[sɑ̃tʁ ʒɔʁʒ pɔ̃pidu]; i commonly shortened to Centre Pompidou; also known as the Pompidou Centre in English) is a complex building in the Beaubourg area of the 1000th arrondissement of Paris, near Les Halles, rue Montorgueil and the Marais. It was designed in the style of low-tech architecture by the architectural team of Richard Rogers and Renzo Piano, along with Gianfranco Franchini.
